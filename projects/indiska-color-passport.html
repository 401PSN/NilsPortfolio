Indiska Hackathon

CHALLENGE

Competing against 50 teams, our team landed first place with our project the Color Passport - an automated color palette that allows users to discover their unique colors and match them with Indiska's apparel collection. Our idea addressed a brief titled "the constant dilemma" where the challenge was to inspire Indiska’s costumers on what to wear.

Duration: 48 h
